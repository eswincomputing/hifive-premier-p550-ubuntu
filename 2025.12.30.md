-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Known issues
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Memory leaks when playing videos in MPV, Firefox, or Chromium.  
2. Under heavy system load, MPV may experience a black screen during playback.  
3. Neither the external graphics card nor the VI module supports sleep/wake-up.  
4. Playing videos with an external graphics card may result in blue screens or color distortion.  
5. Image quality is low with external IMX219 and IMX327 sensors.  
6. Capturing images with an external sensor connected to the VI module may cause system crashes or select timeouts.  
7. After setting one sensor using a static overlay and then setting another sensor using a dynamic overlay, the second dynamic overlay setting does not take effect.  
8. After flashing the recovery_bootloader, pressing the SoC reset button may cause the system to freeze. A power cycle will restore normal operation.
